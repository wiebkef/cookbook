TRAY KEBAB(Turkish Cuisine)

Delicious Meat in Tray,Cooked in WoodFire

  • Mix minced meat, onion, parsley, green pepper, garlic, cumin, salt, black pepper and chili pepper in a bowl or on a tray.

  • When the ingredients are well mixed, spread the minced meat mixture on a round baking tray and flatten it by pressing it with your hands.

  • Slice it to 8 slices with a knife.

  • Pour the melted butter over the kebab and spread it all over with a brush.

  • Mix the pepper paste with water and pour the mixture over it.

  • After arranging the peppers that you have cut in half and the tomatoes you have sliced nicely, bake in a 190 degree oven for 40 minutes and serve hot.


Ingredients Amounts
1 Ground beef 1kg
2 Onion 1 piece
3 Garlic 3 cloves
4 Butter 10gr
5 Tomato 1
6 Red Pepper 1
