Risotto ai funghi

Delicious vegeterian mushroom risotto for two. Easy to prepare. No need for any fancy kitchen helpers to recreate this healthy dish.

Start cooking...

Risotto ai funghi

Risotto ai funghi

Amount Ingredient
300 g variation of mushrooms
200 g Risotto rice
2 onions
1 clove garlic
40 g butter
100 ml white wine
400 ml chicken broth
50 g parmigiano

Clean the mushrooms, cut the large ones into cubes. Peel the shallots and garlic, chop finely and sauté in 1 tbsp butter in a pot and briefly add the rice. Deglaze with white wine and add the chicken stock. Cook the rice for about 12 minutes, stirring constantly, then remove the pan from the heat. Stir in the remaining butter and the Parmesan cheese.

Heat the olive oil in the pan, add the second shallot and sauté the mushrooms for about 3 minutes. Fold the mushrooms into the risotto, season everything with salt, nutmeg and pepper and sprinkle with the chopped parsley.

Tip: As a main course, the quantity is enough for 2 people, as a side dish for 4 people.
